Transport Licensing Appeals Board Meeting

Info:

The main focus of the Transport Licensing Appeals Board meeting was the opportunity to engage with high-ranking officials in the transportation sector. It was an honor to have the chance to meet with the Minister of Transport and his Permanent Secretary. This encounter provided a valuable platform to discuss key agenda items and address pertinent issues within the transportation industry. Additionally, it offered a unique opportunity to gain insights directly from the Minister and his PS, enhancing the effectiveness and impact of the meeting.
