About Me

Hello! I'm James Ngomeli Chief Energising Officer.

I am a seasoned sustainability marketing and energy expert with over 30 years of experience, renowned for driving impactful transformations in the water and energy sectors. With a track record of spearheading large-scale restructuring and strategy reorganization efforts, I have cemented my reputation as a visionary leader committed to sustainable solutions and customer-centric innovation.

Beyond business, I'm a father of five boys and a devoted husband, balancing family life with my professional endeavors. When not driving sustainability, I enjoy honing my skills as an astute golfer and contributing to my community as a proud Rotarian member. With a deep commitment to service, I also hold club memberships that allow me to engage with diverse networks, furthering my impact on both personal and professional fronts. As Executive Chair of Epicenter Africa, I lead a team dedicated to providing innovative water and energy solutions, solidifying my legacy as a catalyst for positive change in the industry.
